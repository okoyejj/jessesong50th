About Jesse Song

Jesse Song is an accomplished leader with over thirty years of transformative leadership and ministry experience. As a senior Minister in London, he has cultivated a profound understanding of guiding diverse communities and empowering them to excel.

In his role at Academy Fellowship International, Jesse spearheads strategic initiatives that promote a holistic approach to youth development—instilling purpose, excellence, and responsible citizenship in every endeavor.

Additionally, as the Executive Director of Cornerstone Counselling Network, he is dedicated to providing expert coaching, mentorship, and counselling on a wide range of issues. His commitment ensures that individuals receive the guidance needed to thrive in all aspects of life.

Jesse is also the visionary behind the innovative Jesse Song Plaza—a digital hub designed to empower individuals through comprehensive leadership development and nation-building programs.

Holding an MBA in International Relations and Diplomacy and having enhanced his extensive experience through courses from prestigious global institutions, Jesse resides in London with his family. His passion and dedication continue to inspire and influence people across continents.
